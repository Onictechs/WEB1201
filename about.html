why does men's mental health matter?

well sadly, 40% of men won't talk about their mental health.

"Traditionally, males have been less inclined to seek help for mental health difficulties." This is likely due to a variety of factors, including stigma and the traditional 'strong guy' image that is still widespread in our society - the notion that displaying emotion is a sign of weakness." -Dr. Natasha Bijlani

suffering in silence.

According to a 2017 report, 60% of sad males would seek treatment, with a stunning 5% using outpatient mental health care. According to the study, males are less likely to take up prescription medicine to enhance their mental health; just 9% of men who seek therapy will use prescription medication to improve their mental health. There aren't enough men in recovery or seeking support to fight mental illness together. That 9% figure is disheartening, because more people should be aware of and desire to pursue mental health. gatewaycounseling.com
